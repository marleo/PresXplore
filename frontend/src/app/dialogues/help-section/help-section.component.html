Video Filtering

  • All/v: Show all keyframes/shots per video (no filtering).
  • First/v: Show only first match per video.

Query Types

  • OCR-Text: database search (case-insensitive) for text in keyframes recognized by Optical Character Recognition (OCR).
  • Speech: database search (case-insensitive) for recognized speech.
  • VideoId: database search (case-insensitive) for a part of their name. Use * to return all (works only for MVK and LHE with First/v!).

Thumbnail Actions

  • Click Thumbnail (or press space) to open preview video summary view.
  • Click Shots (left bottom) to open the video view with shots in a new tab.
  • Click Similar (bottom) to perform a similarity query in a new tab.
  • Click Videoname (right top) to perform a VideoId search for this video.

Video Preview

  • Click crop_square or 1 on the keyboard to show a bigger version of the thumbnail.
  • Click videocam or 3 on the keyboard to show the video starting from the keyframe of the thumbnail.
  • Click Videoname or 0 on the keyboard to perform a VideoId search for this video.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • ← →: select previous or next item (blue border).
  • Space to open/close video summary for selected item.
  • Shift ← →: goto next or previous page. On explore/shot preview it controls the amount of columns rendered.
  • 1-9: goto 1st-9th page.
  • e to extend query (add more words) and q to select entire query for edit.
